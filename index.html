About Me

I design the systems
behind the screens.

I've spent 9.5 years figuring out why people struggle with software — and building the solutions that fix it. From redesigning telematics dashboards for 100,000+ connected vehicles in rural India, to shipping AI-integrated platforms for enterprise teams across four continents, I'm drawn to the hard problems where design meets real stakes.

I don't just design screens. I design the logic behind them — the flows, the systems, the component libraries, and the processes that let teams move fast without breaking things. My work spans B2B SaaS, fintech, life sciences, cybersecurity, and IoT. The common thread is always the same: understand deeply, design decisively, measure honestly.

Right now, I'm exploring how AI reshapes the way designers think, research, and ship. It's the most interesting shift I've seen in a decade of doing this — and I'm building my practice around it.
